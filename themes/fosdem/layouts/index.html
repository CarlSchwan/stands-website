FOSDEM 2021 Stands

Welcome to the FOSDEM 2021 Stands website!

FOSDEM 2021 is our first fully online edition, and FOSDEM would not be FOSDEM without stands; so welcome to our fully online stands display website!

Online stands may seem a bit different from physical stands, but not a lot has changed. We have a little over 50 stands, organised per theme. Normally, we would also split them per building, but this year we decided not to do so ;-)

Each stand has its own page (or group of pages), with all the information and topics they wish to show you. We've also created a special chat room for each stand, where you can discuss with the team behind the project (a bit like the hallways - though less crowded). Some stands also recorded video's, demoing their product or showing their new hot thing.

You can browse the stands per theme here (or below) or visit all the stands here. You can also return to the main conference floor at fosdem.org.
